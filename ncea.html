Level 1 Program

Total Credits: 20

Standards:

  • 92004: Create a Computer Program (5 Credits, Internal)
Purpose: Students are able to create a computer program.
Creating Computer Programs: This standard aims to teach Students how to design, code, test, and document basic computer programs. This involves understanding core concepts like: variables, input/output, and conditional logic.
More Details Here

  • 92005: Develop a digital technology outcome (5 Credits, Internal)
Purpose: Students are able to develop a digital technology outcome.
Developing Digital Technology Outcomes: This standard focuses on utilising digital tools effectively to create a specific outcome. The students will explore the purpose, users, and requirements of their outcomes, as well as doing basic testing.
More Details Here

  • 92006: Demonstrate understanding of usability in human-computer interfaces (5 Credits, External)
Purpose: Students are able to develop a digital technology outcome.
Understanding Usability in Human-Computer Interfaces: The standard aims to teach students how to design digital interfaces effectively with user-friendly considerations in mind. This involves learning about key usability principles.
More Details Here

  • 92007: Design a digital technology outcome (5 Credits, External)
Purpose: Students are able to design a digital technology outcome.
Designing Digital Technology Outcomes: This standard introduces the design process for digital outcomes. Students need to indentify the requirements of the website, make ideas, and describe how they have met specific requirements.
More Details Here
