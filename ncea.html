Level 1 Programme

Total Credits: 20

Standards:

  • 92004: Create a Computer Programme (5 Credits, Internal)
Purpose: Students are able to create a computer programme.
Creating Computer Programmes: This standard aims to teach Students how to deisgn, code, test, and document basic computer programmes. This involves understanding core concepts like: variables, input/output, and conditional logic.
More Details Here

  • 92005: Develop a digital technologies outcome (5 Credits, Internal)
Purpose: Students are able to develop a digital technologies outcome.
Developing Digital Technologies Outcomes: This standard focuses on utilising digital tools effectively to create a specific outcome. The students will explore the purpose, users, and requirements of their outcomes, as well as doing basic testing.
More Details Here

  • 92006: Demonstrate understanding of usability in human-computer interfaces (5 Credits, External)
Purpose: Students are able to develop a digital technologies outcome.
Understanding Usability in Human-Computer Inferaces: The standard aims to to teach students how to design digital interfaces effectively with user-friendly considerations in mind. This involves learning about key usability principles.
More Details Here

  • 92007: Design a digital technologies outcome (5 Credits, External)
Purpose: Students are able to design a digital technologies outcome.
Designing Digital Technologies Outcomes: This standard introduces the design process for digital outcomes. Students need to indentify the requirements of the website, make ideas, and describe how they have met specific requirements.
More Details Here
